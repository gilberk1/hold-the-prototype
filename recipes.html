INGREDIENTS

  • 1 avocado
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 3 pieces bacon
  • 1/2 pound spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

INSTRUCTIONS

  • Blend avocado, egg yolk, garlic, and lemon juice in a powerful blender or food processor. Pulse to blend and add heavy cream gradually. Blend the sauce until smooth and set aside.
  • Cut up strips of bacon and cook in a large pan. Set aside.
  • In a large pot, bring water to a boil and cook the spaghetti. Drain and coat with olive oil. Add sauce, bacon, parmesan, and salt/pepper, stirring until well combine. Enjoy!

Avocado Carbonara
Serves 4 people
15 mins
35 mins
Modified for the following lifestyle
Vegetarian
Dairy-Free

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 avocado
  • 2 egg yolk
  • 2/3 cup soy milk & 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 clove garlic
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 6 pieces seitan
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil

INSTRUCTIONS

  • Whisk soy milk and olive oil in a separate bowl.
  • Blend avocado, egg yolk, garlic, and lemon juice in a powerful blender or food processor. Pulse to blend and add soy milk/olive oil gradually. Blend the sauce until smooth and set aside.
  • Cut up strips of seitan and cook in a large pan. Set aside.
  • In a large pot, bring water to a boil and cook the spaghetti. Drain and coat with olive oil. Add sauce, seitan, and salt/pepper, stirring until well combine. Enjoy!
